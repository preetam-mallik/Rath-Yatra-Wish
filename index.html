The Legend of Ratha Yatra

Discover the ancient story and profound significance behind the annual journey of Lord Jagannath, Lord Balabhadra, and Devi Subhadra.

The Ratha Yatra, or the Chariot Festival, is a monumental Hindu festival associated with Lord Jagannath held at Puri in the state of Odisha, India. It is the oldest Ratha Yatra taking place in India and the world.

The festival commemorates the annual journey of Lord Jagannath, his elder brother Lord Balabhadra, and younger sister Devi Subhadra from their home temple to the Gundicha Temple.
